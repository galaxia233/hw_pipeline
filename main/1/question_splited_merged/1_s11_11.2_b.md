## Solution

(b) $\qquad \mathbf {J} ^ {\alpha \beta} (\mathbf {x} ^ {\sigma} + \mathbf {a} ^ {\sigma}) = \int \left(\mathbf {a} ^ {\alpha} \mathbf {T} ^ {\beta \gamma} - \mathbf {a} ^ {\beta} \mathbf {T} ^ {\alpha \gamma}\right) \mathbf {d} ^ {3} \boldsymbol {\Sigma} _ {\gamma} + \mathbf {J} ^ {\alpha \beta} (\mathbf {x} ^ {\sigma}).$
Thus $\mathbf{J}^{\alpha \beta}$ is not invariant under coordinate translations (different angular momentum about different points).
