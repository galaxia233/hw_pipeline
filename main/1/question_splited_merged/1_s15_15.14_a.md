15.14 (a) Show that in general a boost in the spatial direction $\mathbf{e}_{\hat{\mathbf{j}}}$ leaves invariant the physical components of the Riemann tensor $\mathbb{R}_{\hat{\mathbf{t}}\hat{\mathbf{j}}\hat{\mathbf{t}}\hat{\mathbf{j}}}$ which are "parallel" to the boost. This is analogous to the invariance of $\mathbf{E}_{\hat{\mathbf{j}}}$ and $\mathbf{B}_{\hat{\mathbf{j}}}$ for a boost in the $\mathbf{e}_{\hat{\mathbf{j}}}$ direction.
