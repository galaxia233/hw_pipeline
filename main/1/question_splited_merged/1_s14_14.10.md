Problem 14.10. If $\theta$ is the expansion of a fluid, derive the Raychaudhuri equation
$$
\frac {\mathrm {d} \theta}{\mathrm {d} r} = a _ {; a} ^ {\alpha} + 2 \omega^ {2} - 2 \sigma^ {2} - \frac {1}{3} \theta^ {2} - R _ {\alpha \beta} u ^ {\alpha} u ^ {\beta}
$$
where $\omega^2 = \frac{1}{2}\omega_\alpha\beta\omega^\alpha\beta$ , $\sigma^2 = \frac{1}{2}\sigma_\alpha\beta\sigma^\alpha\beta$ , and the notation is the same as in Problem 5.18.

## Solution

Solution 14.10. From the definition of $\theta = \nabla \cdot \mathbf{u}$ we have
$$
\begin{array}{l} \frac {\mathrm {d} \theta}{\mathrm {d} \tau} = u ^ {\beta} \left(u _ {; \alpha} ^ {\alpha}\right); \beta = u ^ {\beta} u _ {; \alpha \beta} ^ {\alpha} = u ^ {\beta} \left(u _ {; \beta \alpha} ^ {\alpha} - R ^ {\alpha} \beta \alpha \gamma u ^ {\gamma}\right) \tag {1} \\ = \mathfrak {u} ^ {\beta} \mathfrak {u} _ {; \beta \alpha} ^ {\alpha} - \mathrm {R} _ {\beta \gamma} \mathfrak {u} ^ {\beta} \mathfrak {u} ^ {\gamma} . \\ \end{array}
$$
But
$$
\begin{array}{l} \mathrm {u} _ {; \beta \alpha} ^ {\alpha} \mathrm {u} ^ {\beta} = (\mathrm {u} _ {; \beta} ^ {\alpha} \mathrm {u} ^ {\beta}) _ {; \alpha} - \mathrm {u} _ {; \beta} ^ {\alpha} \mathrm {u} ^ {\beta} _ {; \alpha} \\ = \mathrm {a} _ {; \alpha} ^ {\alpha} - \left(\omega_ {\beta} ^ {\alpha} + \sigma_ {\beta} ^ {\alpha} + \frac {1}{3} \theta \mathrm {P} _ {\beta} ^ {\alpha} - \mathrm {a} ^ {\alpha} \mathrm {u} _ {\beta}\right) \\ \times \left(\omega^ {\beta} _ {\alpha} + \sigma^ {\beta} _ {\alpha} + \frac {1}{3} \theta \mathrm {P} ^ {\beta} _ {\alpha} - \mathrm {a} ^ {\beta} \mathrm {u} _ {\alpha}\right) \tag {2} \\ \end{array}
$$
where we have used Problem 5.18. Using the symmetry properties of $\omega_{\alpha \beta}$ , $\sigma_{\alpha \beta}$ , and $\mathbf{P}_{\alpha \beta}$ and the fact that they are orthogonal to $\mathbf{u}$ , Equations (1) and (2) imply
$$
\begin{array}{l} \frac {\mathrm {d} \theta}{\mathrm {d} r} = \mathsf {a} _ {; \alpha} ^ {\alpha} - \omega^ {\alpha} \beta^ {\omega \beta} _ {\alpha} - \sigma^ {\alpha} _ {\beta} \sigma^ {\beta} _ {\alpha} - \frac {1}{3} \theta^ {2} - \mathrm {R} _ {\alpha \beta} \mathrm {u} ^ {\alpha} \mathrm {u} ^ {\beta} \\ = a _ {; \alpha} ^ {\alpha} + 2 \omega^ {2} - 2 \sigma^ {2} - \frac {1}{3} \theta^ {2} - R _ {\alpha \beta} u ^ {\alpha} u ^ {\beta}. \\ \end{array}
$$
