Problem 7.20. Write the following expressions in index-free notation:
(e) $\mathsf{W}^{\alpha}_{;\gamma \beta}\mathsf{U}^{\gamma}\mathsf{U}^{\beta} + \mathsf{W}^{\alpha}_{;\gamma}\mathsf{U}^{\gamma}_{;\beta}\mathsf{U}^{\beta} - \mathsf{U}^{\alpha}_{;\beta}\mathsf{W}^{\beta}_{;\gamma}\mathsf{U}^{\gamma}$ .

## Solution

$$
\mathbf {W} ^ {\alpha ; \beta} \mathbf {V} _ {\beta ; \gamma} \mathbf {U} ^ {\gamma} = \mathbf {W} _ {; \beta} ^ {\alpha} (\mathbf {V} ^ {\beta}; \gamma) \mathbf {U} ^ {\gamma}) = \nabla_ {(\nabla_ {\mathbf {U}} \mathbf {V})} \mathbf {W}
$$
(e) Combine the first two terms of the given expression,
$$
(\mathsf {W} _ {; \gamma} ^ {\alpha} \mathsf {U} ^ {\gamma}) _ {; \beta} \mathbf {U} ^ {\beta} - \mathbf {U} _ {; \beta} ^ {\alpha} (\mathsf {W} _ {; \gamma} ^ {\beta} \mathsf {U} ^ {\gamma}) = \nabla_ {\mathbf {U}} (\nabla_ {\mathbf {U}} \mathbf {\bar {W}}) - \nabla_ {(\nabla_ {\mathbf {U}} \mathbf {\bar {W}})} \mathbf {U} = [ \mathbf {U}, \nabla_ {\mathbf {U}} \mathbf {\bar {W}} ] .
$$
