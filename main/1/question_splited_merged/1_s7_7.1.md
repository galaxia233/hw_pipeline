Problem 7.1. Show that the connection coefficients $\Gamma_{\beta \gamma}^{\alpha}$ do not obey the tensor transformation law.

## Solution

Solution 7.1. Let the transformation be given by $\mathbf{e}_{\mu^{\prime}} = \mathbf{L}_{\mu^{\prime}}^{\sigma}\mathbf{e}_{\sigma}$ , then
$$
\begin{array}{l} \nabla_ {\mathbf {e} _ {\beta} ^ {\prime}} \mathbf {e} _ {\alpha} ^ {\prime} = \Gamma_ {\alpha^ {\prime}} ^ {\tau^ {\prime}} \mathbf {e} _ {\tau^ {\prime}} = \nabla_ {(\mathrm {L} _ {\beta^ {\prime}} ^ {\lambda} \mathbf {e} _ {\lambda})} (\mathrm {L} _ {\alpha^ {\prime}} ^ {\mu} \mathbf {e} _ {\mu}) = \mathrm {L} _ {\beta^ {\prime}} ^ {\lambda} \nabla_ {\mathbf {e} _ {\lambda}} (\mathrm {L} _ {\alpha^ {\prime}} ^ {\mu} \mathbf {e} _ {\mu}) \\ = \mathrm {L} ^ {\lambda} _ {\beta^ {\prime}} \left(\mathrm {L} _ {\alpha^ {\prime}, \lambda} ^ {\mu} \mathrm {e} _ {\mu} + \mathrm {L} _ {\alpha^ {\prime}} ^ {\mu} \Gamma_ {\mu \lambda} ^ {\tau} \mathrm {e} _ {\tau}\right) \\ = L ^ {\lambda} \beta^ {\prime} \left(L ^ {\mu} _ {\alpha^ {\prime}, \lambda} L ^ {\tau^ {\prime}} _ {\mu} e _ {\tau^ {\prime}} + L ^ {\mu} _ {\alpha^ {\prime}} L ^ {\tau^ {\prime}} _ {\gamma} \Gamma^ {\gamma} _ {\mu \lambda} e _ {\tau^ {\prime}}\right), \\ \end {array}
$$
and therefore
$$
\Gamma_ {\alpha} ^ {\tau^ {'}} \beta^ {'} = \mathrm {L} _ {\beta^ {'}} ^ {\lambda} \mathrm {L} _ {\alpha^ {'}} ^ {\mu} \mathrm {L} _ {\gamma} ^ {\tau^ {'}} \Gamma_ {\mu \lambda} ^ {\gamma} + \mathrm {L} _ {\beta^ {'}} ^ {\lambda} \mathrm {L} _ {\mu} ^ {\tau^ {'}} \mathrm {L} _ {\alpha^ {'}, \lambda} ^ {\mu}.
$$
The second term in this equation would not be present in a tensor transformation.
