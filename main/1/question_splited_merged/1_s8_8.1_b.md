# Problem 8.1.
(b) To what vector does the one-form $\widetilde{\mathbf{dx}}^1$ correspond?

## Solution

(b) The vector $\mathbf{g}^{1a}\partial/\partial\mathbf{x}^a \equiv \mathbf{g}^{1a}\mathbf{e}_a$ corresponds to $\widetilde{\mathrm{d}}\mathbf{x}^1$ since, for any vector $\mathbf{v}$
$$
\mathbf {v} \cdot \mathbf {g} ^ {1 \alpha} \mathbf {e} _ {\alpha} = \mathbf {v} ^ {\mu} \mathbf {e} _ {\mu} \cdot \mathbf {g} ^ {1 \alpha} \mathbf {e} _ {\alpha} = \mathbf {v} ^ {\mu} \mathbf {g} _ {\mu \alpha} \mathbf {g} ^ {1 \alpha} = \mathbf {v} ^ {\mu} \delta_ {\mu} ^ {1} = \mathbf {v} ^ {1} = <   \widetilde {\mathbf {d x}} ^ {1}, \mathbf {v} >.
$$
