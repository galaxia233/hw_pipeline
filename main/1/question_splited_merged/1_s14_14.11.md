Problem 14.11. In a certain spacetime fluid flows along geodesics with zero shear and expansion. (See Problem 5.18 for the definitions of shear $\sigma_{\alpha \beta}$ and expansion $\theta$ .) Show that the spacetime has a timelike Killing vector.

## Solution

Solution 14.11. Since the fluid flows on geodesics $\mathsf{a}_{\alpha} \equiv \mathsf{u}^{\beta} \mathsf{u}_{\alpha; \beta} = 0$ and since the flow is shear and expansion free $\sigma_{\alpha \beta} = \theta = 0$ . The decomposition of $\nabla \mathbf{u}$ in Problem 5.18 then gives
$$
\mathrm {u} _ {\alpha}; \beta = \omega_ {\alpha} \beta .
$$
But $\omega$ is antisymmetric, so $\mathbf{u}_{(\alpha ;\beta)} = 0$ and hence $\mathbf{u}$ satisfies Killing's equation.
