Problem 7.20. Write the following expressions in index-free notation:
(b) $\mathbf{V}_{; \beta}^{\alpha} \mathbf{U}^{\beta} - \mathbf{U}_{; \beta}^{\alpha} \mathbf{V}^{\beta}$

## Solution

$$
\mathbf {V} _ {; \beta} ^ {\alpha} \mathbf {U} ^ {\beta} - \mathbf {U} _ {; \beta} ^ {\alpha} \mathbf {V} ^ {\beta} = \nabla_ {\mathbf {U}} \mathbf {V} - \nabla_ {\mathbf {V}} \mathbf {U} \equiv [ \mathbf {U}, \mathbf {V} ] \tag {b}
$$
