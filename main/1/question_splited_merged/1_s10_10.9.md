10.9. In flat Minkowski spacetime find ten Killing vectors that are linearly independent.

## Solution

Solution 10.9. Choose a coordinate system in which the metric is $\eta_{\mu \nu}$ (all of the Christoffel symbols vanish). Then Killing's equation for a Killing vector $\pmb{\xi}$ becomes
$$
2 \xi_ {(\mu ; \nu)} = \xi_ {\mu , \nu} + \xi_ {\nu , \mu} = 0. \tag {1}
$$
We now enumerate and classify linearly independent solutions of Equation (1):
(i) Translation Killing vectors:
$$
\xi_ {i} ^ {\mu} = a _ {i} ^ {\mu} \quad i = 1 - 4 \tag {2}
$$
where $\mathbf{a}_{\mathrm{i}}$ are constant vectors.
(ii) Rotational Killing vectors:
$$
\xi_ {\mathrm {k}} ^ {0} = 0, \quad \xi_ {\mathrm {k}} ^ {1} = \varepsilon^ {1 \mathrm {k m}} \mathrm {x} _ {\mathrm {m}} \quad \mathrm {k} = 1 - 3. \tag {3}
$$
Check:
$$
\begin{array}{l} \xi_ {\mathbf {k} (\mu , \nu)} = \xi_ {\mathbf {k} (\mathrm {i}, \mathrm {j})} = \mathrm {x} _ {\mathrm {m}, (\mathrm {j}} \varepsilon_ {\mathbf {i}) \mathrm {k m}} \\ = \delta_ {\mathrm {m} (\mathrm {j})} \varepsilon_ {\mathrm {i}) \mathrm {k m}} = - \varepsilon_ {\mathrm {k} (\mathrm {i j})} = 0. \\ \end{array}
$$
(iii) Boost Killing vectors:
$$
\xi_ {\mu} ^ {k} = \delta_ {\mu} ^ {[ 0} x ^ {k ]} k = 1, 3. \tag {4}
$$
Check:
$$
\xi_ {(\mu , \nu)} ^ {\mathrm {k}} = \frac {1}{2} \left[ \delta_ {\mu} ^ {[ 0} \delta_ {\nu} ^ {\mathrm {k} ]} + \delta_ {\nu} ^ {[ 0} \delta_ {\mu} ^ {\mathrm {k} ]} \right] = 0.
$$
There are ten Killing vectors in Equations (2)-(4), given in the coordinate system in which $\mathbf{g}_{\mu \nu} = (-1, 1, 1, 1)$ . In any other coordinate system one can find the components of the $\xi_{i}^{\mu}$ , for $i = 1 - 10$ , by transforming them as vectors.
