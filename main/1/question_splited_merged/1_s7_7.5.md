Problem 7.5. Show that the metric tensor is covariantly constant.

## Solution

Solution 7.5. The calculation of $\mathbf{g}_{\alpha \beta ;\gamma}$ is straightforward in a coordinate basis:
$$
\begin{array}{l} \mathbf {g} _ {\alpha \beta ; \gamma} = \mathbf {g} _ {\alpha \beta , \gamma} - \mathbf {g} _ {\sigma \beta} \Gamma_ {a \gamma} ^ {\sigma} - \mathbf {g} _ {\alpha \sigma} \Gamma_ {\beta \gamma} ^ {\sigma} \\ = \mathrm {g} _ {\alpha \beta , \gamma} - 2 \Gamma_ {(\beta \alpha) \gamma} \\ = \mathrm {g} _ {\alpha \beta , \gamma} - \mathrm {g} _ {\alpha \beta , \gamma} = 0. \\ \end {array}
$$
