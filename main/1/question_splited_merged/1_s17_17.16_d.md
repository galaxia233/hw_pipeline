Problem 17.16 (d) Find the asymptotic form of $\Phi$ for $\mathbf{r} \to \mathbf{r}_{+}$ .

## Solution

(d) As $\Delta \to 0$ , Equation (5) gives
$$
\frac {\mathrm {d} ^ {2} \mathrm {R}}{\mathrm {d r} ^ {* 2}} + \left[ \omega^ {2} - \frac {2 \mathrm {a m} \omega}{2 \mathrm {M r} _ {+}} + \frac {\mathrm {a} ^ {2} \mathrm {m} ^ {2}}{(2 \mathrm {M r} _ {+}) ^ {2}} \right] \mathrm {R} = 0
$$
and hence
$$
\mathrm {R} \sim \mathrm {e} ^ {\pm \mathrm {i} (\omega - \mathrm {m} \omega_ {+}) \mathrm {r} ^ {*}}
$$
where
$$
\omega_ {+} \equiv a / 2 M r _ {+}.
$$
