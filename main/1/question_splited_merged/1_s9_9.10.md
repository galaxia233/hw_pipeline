Problem 9.10. Prove that for any second rank tensor
$$
\mathrm {A} _ {; \mu \nu} ^ {\mu \nu} = \mathrm {A} _ {; \nu \mu} ^ {\mu \nu}.
$$

## Solution

Solution 9.10. Form the tensor
$$
\mathbf {C} _ {\mu \nu} ^ {\alpha \beta} \equiv \mathbf {A} _ {; \mu \nu} ^ {\alpha \beta} - \mathbf {A} _ {; \nu \mu} ^ {\alpha \beta}.
$$
From Problem 9.8 we have
$$
\mathbf {C} _ {\mu \nu} ^ {\alpha \beta} = - \mathbf {A} _ {\sigma \mu \nu} ^ {\sigma \beta} \mathbf {R} _ {\sigma \mu \nu} ^ {\alpha} - \mathbf {A} _ {\sigma \mu \nu} ^ {\alpha \sigma} \mathbf {R} _ {\sigma \mu \nu} ^ {\beta}
$$
so that
$$
\mathbf {C} _ {\mu \nu} ^ {\mu \nu} = - \mathrm {A} _ {\sigma \mu \nu} ^ {\sigma \nu} \mathbf {R} _ {\sigma \mu \nu} ^ {\mu} - \mathrm {A} _ {\sigma \mu \nu} ^ {\mu \sigma} \mathbf {R} _ {\sigma \mu \nu} ^ {\nu} = - \mathrm {A} _ {\sigma \nu} ^ {\sigma \nu} \mathbf {R} _ {\sigma \nu} + \mathrm {A} _ {\sigma \mu} ^ {\mu \sigma} \mathbf {R} _ {\sigma \mu} = 0.
$$
