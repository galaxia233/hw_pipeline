Problem 11.3. Show that a system's intrinsic spin 4-vector $\mathbf{S}_{\alpha}$ is orthogonal to its 4-velocity $\mathbf{u}^{\alpha}$ .

## Solution

Solution 11.3. From the definition of $S_{a}$ ,
$$
\mathsf {u} ^ {\alpha} \mathsf {S} _ {\alpha} = - \frac {1}{2} \varepsilon_ {\alpha \beta \gamma \delta} \mathsf {J} ^ {\beta \gamma} \mathsf {u} ^ {\delta} \mathsf {u} ^ {\alpha} = 0
$$
by antisymmetry of $\varepsilon$ and symmetry of $\mathfrak{u}^{\delta}\mathfrak{u}^{a}$
