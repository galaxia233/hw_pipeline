10.3 (b) Show that a linear combination of Killing vectors with constant coefficients is a Killing vector.

## Solution

(b) If a and b are constants, then
$$
(\mathsf {a u} _ {\alpha} + \mathsf {b v} _ {\alpha}); \beta = \mathsf {a u} _ {\alpha}; \beta + \mathsf {b v} _ {\alpha}; \beta .
$$
Thus if $\mathbf{u}$ and $\mathbf{v}$ satisfy Killing's equation, so does $\mathbf{a}\mathbf{u} + \mathbf{b}\mathbf{v}$ .
