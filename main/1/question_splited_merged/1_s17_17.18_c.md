Problem 17.18 (c) The “Zero Angular Momentum Observers” (ZAMO's) in the Kerr geometry have basis 1-forms
$$
\begin{array}{l} \tilde {\omega} ^ {\hat {t}} = \left| g _ {t t} - \omega^ {2} g _ {\phi \phi} \right| ^ {\frac {1}{2}} \widetilde {d t} \\ \tilde {\omega} ^ {\hat {\phi}} = \left(\mathrm {g} _ {\phi \phi}\right) ^ {\frac {1}{2}} \left(\widetilde {\mathrm {d} \phi} - \omega \widetilde {\mathrm {d t}}\right) \\ \tilde {\boldsymbol {\omega}} ^ {\hat {\mathbf {r}}} = \left(\boldsymbol {\Sigma} / \Delta\right) ^ {\frac {1}{2}} \overline {{\mathrm {d} \mathbf {r}}} \\ \tilde {\boldsymbol {\omega}} ^ {\hat {\theta}} = \Sigma^ {\frac {1}{2}} \widetilde {\mathbf {d} \theta} \\ \end{array}
$$
where $\omega \equiv -\mathbf{g}_{t\phi} / \mathbf{g}_{\phi \phi}$
(c) The 4-velocity of the ZAMO is $\mathbf{u} = \mathbf{e}_{\hat{\mathbf{t}}}$ ; show that $\mathbf{u}$ has zero rotation.

## Solution

(c) The 4-velocity has zero rotation if $\omega_{\alpha}\beta = 0$ (see Problem 5.18) or equivalently if $\mathbf{u}$ is hypersurface orthogonal:
$$
\mathrm {u} _ {[ \alpha ; \beta} \mathrm {u} _ {\gamma ]} = 0
$$
(see Problem 7.23). The quickest way to check this condition is to use forms and the equivalence of $\mathfrak{u}[\hat{\alpha};\hat{\beta}]$ and $\mathbf{d}\tilde{\boldsymbol{\omega}}^{\mathrm{t}}$ . The condition $\mathfrak{u}[\hat{\alpha};\hat{\beta}\mathbf{u}_{\hat{\gamma}}] = 0$ is then equivalent to $\mathbf{d}\tilde{\boldsymbol{\omega}}^{\mathrm{t}}\wedge \tilde{\boldsymbol{\omega}}^{\mathrm{t}} = 0$ . But $\tilde{\boldsymbol{\omega}}^{\mathrm{t}} = \boldsymbol{a}\mathrm{d}t$ where $a = |\mathbf{g}_{\mathrm{tt}} - \omega^{2}\mathbf{g}_{\phi \phi}|^{\frac{1}{2}} = a(\mathbf{r},\theta)$ and thus
$$
\mathrm {d} \tilde {\omega } ^ {\mathrm {t}} = a, _ {\mathrm {r}} \widetilde {\mathrm {d r}} \wedge \widetilde {\mathrm {d} t} + a, _ {\theta} \widetilde {\mathrm {d} \theta} \wedge \widetilde {\mathrm {d} t}
$$
so $\mathbf{d}\widetilde{\boldsymbol{\omega}}^{\mathrm{t}}\wedge \widetilde{\boldsymbol{\omega}}^{\mathrm{t}} = 0$ since $\widetilde{\mathrm{dt}}\wedge \widetilde{\mathrm{dt}} = 0$
