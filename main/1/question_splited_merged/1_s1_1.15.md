Problem 1.15. Write the magnitude of the acceleration measured in the observer's frame as an invariant.

## Solution

Solution 1.15. Since $\mathbf{a} \cdot \mathbf{u} = 0$ , in the observer's local rest frame
$$
\mathbf {a} = (0, \mathbf {a} ^ {\hat {\mathbf {j}}}),
$$
where $\mathbf{a}^{\hat{\mathbf{j}}}$ is the $j^{th}$ component of locally measured acceleration. Then, the squared magnitude of his locally measured acceleration is
$$
\mathbf {a} ^ {2} \equiv \mathbf {a} ^ {\hat {\mathbf {j}}} \mathbf {a} _ {\hat {\mathbf {j}}} = (0, \mathbf {a} ^ {\hat {\mathbf {j}}}) \cdot (0, \mathbf {a} ^ {\hat {\mathbf {j}}}) = \mathbf {a} \cdot \mathbf {a}.
$$
